About Us

AweChords is an a cappella group at UCLA. Originally named “Nhip-Am”, it was formed in 2006 as a choir for the annual Vietnamese Culture Night. Since then, the group has expanded to more diverse audiences throughout Los Angeles and in 2010 regrouped to become the AweChords.

This newly formed co-ed a cappella group was founded to expand each of its members' musical horizons and to extend to the public a joy for music. In exploring the versatility of their voices and the creative license that comes with arranging/composing, this group strives to further their knowledge of music and apply that knowledge serve the greater LA community.
