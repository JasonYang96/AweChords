Hey there!

AweChords A Cappella is a co-ed group at UCLA that strives to share our passion for music with the greater Los Angeles community.
AweChords was founded in 2006 as a musical component in UCLAÊ¼s annual Vietnamese Culture Night. In 2010, we began to diversify our sound and regrouped to form AweChords A Cappella.
Our repertoire ranges from pop to rock to show tunes, and we are constantly working on new pieces to share with our audiences.
Above all, AweChords is a home away from home. We aim to create life-long bonds that will last years after we have finished our last performance.
